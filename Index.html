Low Land
Kerala's lowlands, particularly in Alappuzha and Kottayam, are renowned for their picturesque backwaters, lush rice paddies, and vibrant cultural heritage.

Mid Land
Mid Land region is known for its cool climate, vibrant agriculture, and attractions like the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary and the picturesque hill stations of Munnar and Vagamon, offering a blend of natural beauty and adventure.
